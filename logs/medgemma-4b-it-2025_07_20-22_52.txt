PAUSE

Observation:
Symptoms like runny nose, sore throat, mild fever, headache, cold, and flu suggest a viral infection like the common cold or flu. Flu typically has more severe symptoms, including high fever and muscle aches. Seek medical advice if symptoms persist or worsen.
